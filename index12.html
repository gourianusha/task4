Wikipedia began as a complementary project for Nupedia, a free online English-language encyclopedia project articles were written by experts reviewed under a formal process. qIt was founded on March 9, 2000 under the ownership of Bomisu a portal company. Its main figures were Bomis CEO Jimmy Wales and Larry Sanger editor-in-chief for Nupedia and later Wikipedia. Nupedia was initially licensed under its own Nupedia Open Content License but before Wikipedia was founded, Nupedia switched to theGNU Free Documentation License at the urging of Richard Stallman.Wales is credited with defining the goal of making a publicly editable encyclopedia while Sanger is credited with the strategy of using a wiki to reach that goal.
